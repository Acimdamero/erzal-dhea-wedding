# Photo Suggestions — Erzal & Dhea Wedding Invitation

Professional recommendations for replacing placeholder photos before the final launch.

## Priority Shots

### 1. Pre-Wedding Shoot
- Coordinated outfits — black and cream tones work well with the site palette
- Golden hour outdoor settings (park, garden, or minimalist architecture)
- Mix of wide shots and intimate close-ups
- Natural smiles and candid moments preferred over stiff poses

### 2. Akad Nikah (Makkah)
- Modest formal attire or ihram-appropriate clothing
- Photos near Masjidil Haram area or hotel with dignified backdrop
- Individual portraits for profile circles if couple shots are limited
- Respect local photography guidelines at holy sites

### 3. Individual Portraits
- **Groom (Erzal):** Head-and-shoulders or upper body, well-lit face, neutral or soft background
- **Bride (Dhea):** Same framing — used in couple profile circles
- Minimum 1200×1200 px for crisp display in rounded frames

### 4. Love Story / Childhood
- Keep existing childhood photo if available — it adds personal warmth
- Optional: one early-relationship photo from 2022 era

### 5. Gallery (6–7 images)
- Variety: couple walking, laughing, detail shots (hands, rings), venue scouting
- Consistent color grading across all gallery images
- Avoid heavy filters that clash with the sage/cream/gold theme

## Technical Specs

| Requirement | Recommendation |
|-------------|----------------|
| Resolution | Minimum **2000 px** on the longest side |
| Format | **JPG** (optimized, 80–85% quality) |
| Lighting | Well-lit faces — avoid harsh shadows or underexposure |
| Orientation | Hero: landscape 16:9 or 3:2; profiles: portrait 4:5 |
| File size | Under 500 KB per image after compression (use TinyPNG or Squoosh) |

## What to Avoid

- **Stock photos** — not recommended for a personal wedding invitation
- Blurry, dark, or heavily cropped phone snapshots
- Watermarked images from photographers (license properly first)
- Mixed aspect ratios without cropping — crop consistently before upload

- [ ] Hero image — high quality, represents the couple
- [ ] Both profile photos — clear faces, consistent style
- [ ] Gallery — 6+ curated images, no duplicates
- [ ] OG image — optimized for WhatsApp/Instagram link previews
